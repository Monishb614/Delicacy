Our Team

At Delicacy, our commitment to excellence is driven by the synergy of our dedicated and skilled team members. We understand that every role, from the front of house to the kitchen, plays an integral part in crafting an unforgettable dining experience for our guests.


We are a pioneering specialty food retailer and importer that began with a modest shop in the now-iconic North Philadelphia Italian Market.In summary, teamwork at Delicacy is the heartbeat of our establishment. It's the synergy of diverse talents, shared goals, and unwavering dedication that elevates us from being merely a restaurant to being a culinary destination that offers not only exceptional food but also an unparalleled dining experience.
